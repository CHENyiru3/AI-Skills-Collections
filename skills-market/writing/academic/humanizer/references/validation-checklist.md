# Validation Checklist

Use this file for high-stakes text, final QA, or when the rewrite still feels
too polished or artificial.

## Quick pass

Check for:

- obvious AI transitions still present
- repeated sentence shapes
- inflated importance or generic uplift
- vague authorities like "experts say" without attribution
- tone mismatch for the genre

If two or more remain, revise again.

## Deeper pass

### Vocabulary

- are there clusters of AI-marked words close together?
- did jargon survive where plain language would work?
- does the wording sound specific rather than impressive?

### Structure

- do multiple sentences share the same shape?
- do paragraphs keep restating their own point?
- are there too many lists of three or mirrored contrasts?

### Voice

- does the text sound like a person with a reason to say it?
- is the level of personality appropriate for the genre?
- is the tone controlled rather than sterile?

### Academic safety

For academic prose, verify:

- claims remain evidence-compatible
- terminology remains intact
- justified hedging was preserved
- no casual phrasing was introduced

## Final adversarial prompt

Ask yourself:

1. "What still makes this sound AI-generated?"
2. "If I removed only the highest-signal tells, what would I change?"

Then make the smallest necessary final revision.
